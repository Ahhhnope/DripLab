Our Heritage

Founded in 1994 in a small brick-and-mortar corner of the city, Drip Lab began as a singular pursuit of the perfect pour-over. What started with a vintage copper kettle and a passion for Ethiopian Yirgacheffe has grown into a community of coffee enthusiasts dedicated to the craft.

We spent years traveling to remote highlands, building direct relationships with farmers who share our respect for the soil. Today, every cup we serve carries the history of those journeys and the legacy of our first small shop.
